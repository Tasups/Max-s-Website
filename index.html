Max's Blossom Boutique

About Me

My name is Maxine VanSlyke. I am originally from Rochester, NY and then migrated to a small upstate NY town called Fort Plain. I moved to Asheville in 2012 where I fell in love with the local arts. I have always enjoyed working with glass as a medium and have enjoyed doing stained glass in the past. My current creations are a meld of new and antique plates created to resemble flowers for your home and garden. Enjoy your treasure from Max's Bloom Boutique.
